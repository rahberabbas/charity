Our Mafalala Project

We have been funding the infrastructure of classrooms toilets and drinking water sources where needed in the region of Mafalala in Maputo/Mozambique.

Our primarily aim which is to build classrooms and rehabilitate existing ones inside schools in the area with the structure and sanitary facilities necessary.
A solid and permanent building structure which allows the children to learn in a safe physical environment, to relieve the opposed poor and rough circumstances currently being encountered.

About Us

Mafalala is one of the oldest in the capital, Maputo. Located in the centre-west, Mozaid funds the building of classrooms in communities there where the infrastructure is inadequate or non- existing.

Our Motive

Please help us to help children recover and build a new future. A small amount can make a big change. Education is the best way out of poverty.
